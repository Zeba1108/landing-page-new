Order vs Received Analysis

What you're seeing

A side-by-side comparison screen that matches your purchase order against what you actually received from the supplier. Each line item shows ordered quantities versus received quantities, highlighting any discrepancies instantly.

What problems it catches

  • • Missing items that were ordered but not delivered
  • • Quantity mismatches (ordered 100, received 80)
  • • Pack size changes (ordered strips, received bottles)
  • • Extra items delivered that weren't in the order
  • • Wrong product substitutions

How to read this screen

  • • Green highlights indicate perfect matches
  • • Red or yellow highlights flag discrepancies
  • • Compare the "Ordered" and "Received" columns side-by-side
  • • Click on any flagged item to see details and take action

Why this matters

  • • Prevents billing errors before they enter your system
  • • Catches supplier mistakes immediately, not weeks later
  • • Reduces inventory discrepancies and stock confusion
  • • Saves time by flagging issues at receipt, not during reconciliation
  • • Protects your pharmacy from financial losses from missing or wrong items
